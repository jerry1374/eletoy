**Specifications:**

Interface: USB to Audio/Serial Converter

Compatibility: KENWOODs, YAESU, ICOM radios

Signal Levels: TTL/232/CIV supported

USB Sound Card Chip: DP108 (mature solution)

Dimensions: 53×24×14mm (excluding USB connector)

Connector: Dual 3.5mm 4PIN plugs Voltage

Output: 3.3V (e.g., for LAB599 TX500)

**Features:**

1）2-in-1 Design: Combines audio and serial control interfaces for seamless radio connectivity.

2）Multi-Protocol Support: Works with TTL/232/CIV levels, compatible with major radio brands.

3）PTT Control via USB: Enables RTS-based PTT for FT8, JT65, PSK, and other HAM apps.

4）Compact & Portable: Mini size (53×24×14mm) for easy installation and space-saving.

5）Flexible Power Option: Provides 3.3V output to power devices like LAB599 TX500.
