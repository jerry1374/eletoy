## Overview

AUDIO2RIG is an interface device for audio and PTT control conversion between
computers and radio stations, with audio transformer and optocoupler isolation.

Compatible with Kenwood, Yaesu, ICOM and more. Supports VOX PTT control; audio uses USB.
