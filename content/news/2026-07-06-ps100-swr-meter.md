Web tools for PS100,support android and PC.    [https://docs.eletoy.cc/PS100/](https://docs.eletoy.cc/PS100/)

## Features

• Real-time forward / reflected power and SWR measurement 

• Five display screens, cycled by a single button  

• Peak Envelope Power (PEP) hold and average-power statistics  

• Power-vs-time trend graph with auto-ranging full scale 

• Graphical battery indicator, low-battery warning and auto power-off 

 • Adjustable-threshold high-SWR audible/visible alarm 

 • Tuning-aid tone (pitch tracks SWR — helps tune an external manual ATU by ear) 

• Continuous-transmit timeout reminder  

• 9600-baud serial data output and remote configuration  （built in Bluetooth）

• 24-point power calibration
